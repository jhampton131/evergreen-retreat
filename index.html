About Evergreen

A space to step away from the noise

Evergreen is a space for women who long to step away from the hurry and constant demands of everyday life — to find rest for their souls. Women of all ages gather in a small, meaningful community to slow down, reflect, and reconnect with what truly matters.

We believe an evergreen life is possible — not because life is always easy, but because we trust in the One who is everlasting and unchanging. When our roots grow deep in Him, we remain steady, nourished, and alive even in difficult seasons.

Evergreen exists to create sacred spaces for women to rest, grow, and be renewed.
